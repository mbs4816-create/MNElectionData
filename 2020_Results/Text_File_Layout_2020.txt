Office of the Minnesota Secretary of State (OSS)

2020 State General Election Results
----------------------------------------------------

 - The filenames given below are part of the URLs on the Election Night Reporting website and used on the ftp site.
 - The files on OSS web and ftp sites contain ASCII text, separated by semicolons. 
 - Information in the supporting files is subject to correction and change up until election day.
 - Election results may change after being reported initially on election night.
 - Files with precinct-level results will not be available until the morning after election day.
 - For questions, contact OSS Elections Division at elections.dept@state.mn.us

Last revised 11/30/2020

----------------------------------------------------
Election Results Files
----------------------------------------------------

Results are formatted in flat files which have one row for each candidate and question response in each geographic area 
(statewide, county, district, or precinct, depending on the file).

Election Results (various names)

	State
	County ID (if applicable)
	Precinct name (if applicable)
	Office ID
	Office Name 
	District* 
	Candidate Order Code
	Candidate Name (First/Last/Suffix all in one field)
	Suffix (not used)
	Incumbent Code (not used)
	Party Abbreviation**
	Number of Precincts reporting
	Total number of precincts voting for the office
	Votes for Candidate
	Percentage of Votes for Candidate out of Total Votes for Office
	Total number of votes for Office in area

*Note: The District field is used for U.S. Representative, State Senate, State Representative, and County Commissioner districts; District Court; school district numbers; municipal FIPS codes; soil and water conservation district codes, and hospital district codes. The District field is not used for statewide offices (U.S. President, U.S. Senate, Governor, Secretary of State, State Auditor, Attorney General, Supreme Court, Court of Appeals), county-wide offices, or city wards. 
**Note: All local candidates are non-partisan.

Recount Results (various names)

	State
	County ID (if applicable)
	Precinct number (if applicable)
	Office ID
	Office Name 
	Candidate ID
	Candidate Name (First/Last/Suffix all in one field)
	Party Abbreviation
	Number of Precincts reporting
	Total number of precincts voting for the office
	Votes for Candidate (as canvassed)
	Votes for Candidate (as recounted)
	Percentage of Votes for Candidate out of Total Votes for Office (as recounted)
	Total number of votes for Office in area (as recounted)
	Number of ballots challenged by Candidate in area

Note: if there are multiple recounts they will all be in the same file.
